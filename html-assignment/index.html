About Me

My name is Nathan Morakinyo. I am a Nigerian-Canadian. My parents migrated to Canada in the year 2000, the same year I was born. I was born in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. I lived in several parts in Canada. I live in Edmonton, Yellowknife, Iqualuit and Currently in Ottawa. I have a very lovely family commprizing of my Dad, Mom, My three siblings, and myself.

I have obtained my high school diploma from Ottawa Secondary School (OTSS). I also did additional credit courses with Virtual High School (VHS). I am currently a student of Algonquin College studying Interactive Media Design (IMD). I love, basically, all the courses I am doing under my current program.
